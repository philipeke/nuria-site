What we believe

Constraints we chose. And you can hold us to.

Your deen is not a product.

The Quran is free. Prayer times are free. The Qibla, the dhikr, the duas. Free. Always. We will never put a paywall between a Muslim and their prayer.

No Muslim locked out.

Not by wealth, language, or tradition. Nuria serves every Muslim. Equally. No tradition-based gating, no quiet decision about who counts.

Your madhab. Your way.

Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, Hanbali. Treated as living traditions, not a dropdown filter you tolerate. Your fiqh is how you pray. So Nuria meets you there.

Precision is an act of worship.

Prayer times are one of the five pillars. Nuria calculates yours by your exact location, every day. When you stand before Allah, you should not be guessing whether you are on time.

AI is held to a higher standard than we hold ourselves.

When a machine speaks about the deen, it must be more careful than any of us would be. Not less. NuriaOne cites its sources, admits what it does not know, and never invents a fatwa.

About the Product Owner

Adam is the product lead behind Nuria, a project built from personal faith, lived experience, and a clear purpose.

He embraced Islam in adulthood at the Bosnian mosque in Gothenburg, where his reversion was guided by Imam Fuad Colic. However, his connection to Islam goes further back: he has lived closely connected to the faith since the age of 16, shaped both by personal experience and the Muslim community around him.

With a background as a long-time entrepreneur, Adam has built and led several successful ventures. Yet Nuria stands apart from everything else he has done.

Nuria was not created as just another app. It was built out of a real need: a need for something simpler, clean and ad-free, secure, more beautiful, and more meaningful. An app designed by a Muslim, for Muslim needs, shaped by Islam and refined together with other Muslims.

The project has been developed alongside a close circle of fellow believers, together with his lifelong brother Philip, with whom he runs a technology studio.

Together, they share one intention: to build something of real value for the ummah.

Nuria is the result of that intention. A focused effort to create an experience that helps Muslims stay connected to their faith, every day.
