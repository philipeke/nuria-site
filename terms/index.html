9. Hiba Gift Subscriptions

9.1 What Hiba is

"Hiba" (هبة, gift) is a one-time gift purchase that allows you (the "Buyer") to give a designated recipient (the "Recipient") one (1) year of Barakah tier access. The Hiba product identifier is nuria_hiba_yearly_gift. The price is set by Apple or Google in your local currency and presented at checkout.

9.2 How Hiba works

  • At checkout, the Buyer identifies the Recipient by Nuria code or by the email address associated with the Recipient's Nuria account.
  • Apple or Google completes the store-side purchase first.
  • The purchase is not finalised by Nuria until our backend verifies the Recipient's eligibility (e.g. that the email or code corresponds to an existing Nuria account and the Recipient is eligible to receive the gift).
  • Upon successful verification, the Recipient's Nuria account is credited with one (1) year of Barakah tier access starting from the time of crediting. The Recipient receives an in-App notification.
  • If verification fails (for example because the email does not match a Nuria account or because the Recipient already holds an active Barakah subscription that would be displaced unfairly), we will use reasonable efforts to (a) reach out to the Buyer to correct the recipient details, or (b) request a refund through the relevant store on the Buyer's behalf.

9.3 Limitations and refunds for Hiba

  • A Hiba gift is non-recurring — it provides one year of Barakah access and does not auto-renew.
  • A Hiba gift is non-transferable once delivered.
  • Refunds for Hiba purchases are handled by Apple or Google per their policies, subject to the Recipient not having materially consumed the year of access.
  • If the Recipient already has an active Barakah subscription, the Hiba year may be queued to begin upon expiry of the current subscription, or the purchase may be refunded, at our discretion and consistent with platform rules.
