By granting location permission you consent to the App accessing your device's location for the purposes described above. Revoking permission disables the Qibla and Prayer Times features but does not affect any other App functionality.

Prayer times and Qibla direction are calculated using standard astronomical and geographic formulae based on your device's reported coordinates.
