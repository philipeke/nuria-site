The offer

Built for sincere sharing, with clear economics.

Nuria rewards ambassadors when a referred user subscribes with their code. Earnings are calculated from Net Revenue: the amount Nuria receives after the Apple App Store or Google Play platform fee has been deducted.

Your ambassador share continues every billing cycle for as long as the referred subscription stays active, subject to the terms document.

Four simple steps from code to recurring rewards.

Key terms

The details that matter before you share.

Net Revenue basis

The 50% share is calculated after Apple or Google platform fees have been deducted.

Payout threshold

Balances carry forward until the Accrued Balance reaches EUR 100.

Transfer fees

Bank, SWIFT, PayPal, or Wise fees may be passed through at cost and shown on the payout statement.

Independent taxes

Ambassadors are independent and responsible for declaring earnings in their own country.
