Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and handle your data with honesty, care, and transparency.

Effective date: 1 January 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OakDev & AI AB ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Nuria mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

1. Who We Are

Nuria is published by OakDev & AI AB, a company registered in Sweden. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@oakdev.ai

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a display name (optional).
  • Subscription information: Purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card details. We receive confirmation of your purchase and subscription status.
  • Support communications: If you contact us for support, we retain the content of your messages and your email address to assist you.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: We collect information about how you use the App, such as which features you access, reflections you request, and how frequently you use the App. This helps us improve the experience.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier for technical and security purposes.
  • Crash reports: If the App crashes, anonymised diagnostic information is collected to help us fix issues.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera data, or any sensitive personal data unrelated to your use of the App. We do not sell your data to third parties, ever.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Nuria app and its features.
  • Verify your account and manage your subscription (Standard or Barakah).
  • Personalise your daily reflections and spiritual content.
  • Respond to your support requests and communications.
  • Improve the App through analysis of aggregated, anonymised usage data.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: To fulfil our obligations to you as a user, including providing the App and managing your subscription.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve the App, ensure security, and prevent fraud.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law.
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly provided consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Apple / Google: For processing in-app purchases and subscription management.
  • Infrastructure providers: Trusted cloud service providers who host the App backend, subject to strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you with the App. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@oakdev.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Nuria is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including Sweden and countries where our infrastructure providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers, including standard contractual clauses where required.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@oakdev.ai
  • Support: nuria.app/support

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority if you believe we have not handled your data appropriately.
