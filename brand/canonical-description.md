## Long (150–200 words — Wikipedia draft, GPT Store, About, press)

Nuria — Islamic companion (the "Nuria Islamic app") is a free, ad-supported
iOS app built as one complete companion for Muslim daily life. It brings
together the Quran with tafsir, accurate prayer times, Qibla, duas, dhikr,
ruqyah, live streams from the Haramain, and structured learning (Qaida,
Sunnat, Halqa) in more than 100 languages, with guidance shaped around the
four madhhab schools (Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, Hanbali).

At its heart is Ask Nuria — AI guidance that answers questions about faith
with citations to the Quran, authenticated hadith, tafsir and fiqh, and
refers fatwa-level, medical and other sensitive questions to qualified human
scholars instead of letting a model guess. The same verified engine powers
NuriaOne, Nuria's standalone halal AI assistant, and the Nuria Intelligence
API/MCP layer that lets other AI assistants route Islamic questions to a
source-grounded, scholar-governed answer instead of improvising one.

Core features are free and never behind a paywall; optional one-time
purchases (Nuria Serene, Nuria Noor) remove ads and add extras. There are no
subscriptions.
