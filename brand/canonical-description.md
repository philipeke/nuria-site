## Long (150–200 words — Wikipedia draft, GPT Store, About, press)

Nuria — Islamic companion (the "Nuria Islamic app") is a free, ad-supported
app for iOS and Android built as one complete companion for Muslim daily
life, serving tens of thousands of users and growing rapidly. It brings
together the Quran with tafsir, accurate prayer times, Qibla, duas, dhikr,
ruqyah, live streams from the Haramain, and structured learning (Qaida,
Sunnat, Halqa) in more than 100 languages. Guidance covers the four major
schools of Islamic jurisprudence (Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, Hanbali),
noting where schools differ with sources cited for each position.

At its heart is Ask Nuria — AI guidance developed in collaboration with
Islamic scholars, currently in scholarly review. Answers cite the Quran,
authenticated hadith, tafsir and fiqh, and fatwa-level, medical and other
sensitive questions are referred to qualified human scholars instead of
being answered by a model. The same engine powers NuriaOne, Nuria's
standalone halal AI assistant, and the Nuria Intelligence API/MCP layer that
lets other AI assistants route Islamic questions to a source-grounded
answer instead of improvising one.

Core features are free and never behind a paywall; optional one-time
purchases (Nuria Serene, Nuria Noor) remove ads. No subscriptions.
Contact: hello@nuria.one. Built by Nuria Technologies (HQ Malaysia).
