About Nuria

A Companion of Light for Every Soul

Nuria — meaning luminous — brings the timeless teachings of Islam into the rhythm of your daily life. It is free to download and built as one complete companion for Quran, duas, prayer, learning, reflections, and spiritual growth rooted in Qur'an and authentic Islamic teaching.
