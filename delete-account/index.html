Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. You may request permanent deletion of your Nuria account and all associated data at any time.

📱

Delete in the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside Nuria: go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and follow the steps.

✉️

Request via Email

You can also email us directly at hello@nuria.one with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will process it within 30 days.

Submit a Deletion Request

Use the form below to submit a formal account deletion request. Please provide the email address linked to your Nuria account.

Please read before continuing:
Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted:
  • All your account data and preferences will be permanently removed.
  • Your reflection history will be deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • Your Quran notes, Halqa participation records, Qaida progress, Sunnat practice choices, ruqyah playback preferences, scholar-path defaults, achievements, and Luxury Stone preferences tied to your account will be removed where they are stored on Nuria systems.
  • If you have active recurring billing for Nuria through Apple or Google (legacy accounts only), cancel it in the store before deleting your account. Serene and Noor one-time purchases do not need cancellation.
  • Unused reflections and Serene or Noor benefits do not transfer to a new account.
Cancel & Go Back

What Happens Next

After we receive your request:

  • We will send a confirmation email to the address you provided.
  • Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
  • During this period, you can cancel the deletion request by contacting hello@nuria.one.
  • After 30 days, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.
  • Version 4.3 feature records, including learning progress, theme preferences, personalized settings, achievements, partner/referral links tied to your account, and support correspondence connected to the deletion request, are reviewed under the same privacy and retention rules.
  • Some anonymised, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for statistical purposes only.

Need Help Instead?

Before you go, we would love to help if something isn't working as expected. Please reach out to us at hello@nuria.one or visit our Support Center — we're here for you, in shaa Allah.
