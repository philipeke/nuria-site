Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. You may request permanent deletion of your Nuria account and all associated data at any time.

📱

Delete in the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside Nuria: go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and follow the steps.

✉️

Request via Email

You can also email us directly at hello@oakdev.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will process it within 30 days.

Submit a Deletion Request

Use the form below to submit a formal account deletion request. Please provide the email address linked to your Nuria account.

Please read before continuing:
Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted:
  • All your account data and preferences will be permanently removed.
  • Your reflection history will be deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • If you have an active Barakah subscription, please cancel it via your App Store or Google Play settings before deleting your account — we are unable to issue refunds for any unused subscription time.
  • Unused credits and any Barakah supporter benefits do not transfer to a new account.
Cancel & Go Back

What Happens Next

After we receive your request:

  • We will send a confirmation email to the address you provided.
  • Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
  • During this period, you can cancel the deletion request by contacting hello@oakdev.app.
  • After 30 days, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.
  • Some anonymised, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for statistical purposes only.

Need Help Instead?

Before you go, we would love to help if something isn't working as expected. Please reach out to us at hello@oakdev.app or visit our Support Center — we're here for you, in shaa Allah.
