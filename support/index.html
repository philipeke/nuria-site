About OakDev & AI

Nuria is built with care by OakDev & AI AB, a small software company based in Uddevalla, Sweden. We are a team of developers and designers with a deep interest in faith and spirituality — and Nuria was founded to honour our dear Muslim friend in the studio, whose love for Islam inspired everything you see here.

Our mission is to create thoughtful, beautiful digital tools that serve the Muslim community with sincerity and respect. Every feature, every word, and every design decision is made with your spiritual journey in mind.

About the Product Owner

Adam is the product lead behind Nuria, a project built from personal faith, lived experience, and a clear purpose.

He embraced Islam in adulthood at the Bosnian mosque in Gothenburg, where his reversion was guided by Imam Fuad Colic. However, his connection to Islam goes further back: he has lived closely connected to the faith since the age of 16, shaped both by personal experience and the Muslim community around him.

With a background as a long-time entrepreneur, Adam has built and led several successful ventures. Yet Nuria stands apart from everything else he has done.

Nuria was not created as just another app. It was built out of a real need: a need for something simpler, clean and ad-free, secure, more beautiful, and more meaningful. An app designed by a Muslim, for Muslim needs, shaped by Islam and refined together with other Muslims.

The project has been developed alongside a close circle of other believers: his Pakistani Muslim brother Akmal and sister Bushra, who came to Sweden in 2020, and his lifelong brother Philip, with whom he runs a technology studio.

Together, they share one intention: to build something of real value for the ummah.

Nuria is the result of that intention. A focused effort to create an experience that helps Muslims stay connected to their faith, every day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is a Sunni Islamic guidance app that brings together daily scripture, role models, personal reflections, Ask Nuria, Qibla, prayer times, notifications, a digital tasbeeh, and a monthly prayer calendar — all rooted in authentic Sunni tradition.

How does Nuria work? Is it free?

⌄

Nuria is free to download. Every new account begins with 50 starter credits. Daily guidance, Qibla, prayer times, notifications, tasbeeh, and the prayer calendar are available in the app, while personalised reflections and Ask Nuria use credits. Barakah is the optional subscription for users who want to support the development team and help us keep improving Nuria.

What are credits?

⌄

Credits are the balance used for personalised reflections and Ask Nuria answers. Each reflection request or Ask Nuria question uses one credit, and your balance is always visible inside the app.

What is Barakah and how do I get it?

⌄

Barakah (بركة — meaning blessing) is Nuria's optional support subscription. It is for users who want to support the development team so we can maintain the app, improve the experience, and keep building what comes next. Any included credits or supporter perks are shown clearly in the app and in your store before you subscribe.

How many credits do I get?

⌄

Every new account starts with 50 starter credits. If you later choose Barakah, any additional credits or supporter benefits are shown in the app before purchase.

Is the content based on authentic Sunni scholarship?

⌄

Yes. Every piece of content in Nuria is grounded in the Holy Quran, the Hadith of the Prophet ﷺ, and the authentic tradition of Sunni Islam, delivered with care, warmth, and respect for the faith.

What happens when I run out of credits?

⌄

You can keep using Nuria's daily guidance and prayer tools as normal. If you want more credits for personalised reflections or Ask Nuria, any Barakah subscription details and supporter perks are shown clearly in the app before you subscribe.

How do I delete my account?

⌄

You can request account deletion from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or by visiting our account deletion page. We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

On which devices can I use Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is available on iOS via the Apple App Store and on Android via Google Play.

I found an issue or have a suggestion. How do I reach you?

⌄

Use the contact form below or email us at hello@oakdev.app. We read every message and strive to respond within 1–2 business days, in shaa Allah.
