Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is a daily Sunni Islamic guidance and reflection app. Each day you receive a handcrafted prayer, a verse from the Quran or Hadith, a role model from Islamic history, and personal spiritual reflections across 15 different categories — all rooted in authentic Sunni tradition.

How does Nuria work? Is there a subscription?

⌄

Nuria is a paid app. When you download it, you receive 150 reflections included with your purchase, along with daily scripture, a daily role model, and a daily personal reflection. There is no mandatory subscription — you can use those 150 reflections at your own pace. When you want more, you can purchase Barakah or buy top-ups.

What are reflections?

⌄

Reflections are personalised spiritual texts you request from Nuria across any of the 15 spiritual categories — such as Morning Prayer, Gratitude, Strength & Patience, and more. Each reflection is uniquely crafted to speak to your heart and situation at that moment. Each request uses one reflection from your balance.

What is Barakah and how do I get it?

⌄

Barakah (بركة — meaning blessing) is Nuria's premium in-app purchase. It adds 300 reflections to your balance on top of your Standard allowance, unlocks all 15 reflection categories, and gives you priority access to upcoming features. You can purchase Barakah directly inside the app on iOS or Android. Once your Barakah reflections are used, you can also buy top-ups of additional reflections whenever you need more.

How many reflections do I get in total?

⌄

When you purchase Nuria you receive 150 reflections included with the app. If you then purchase Barakah, you receive an additional 300 reflections — giving you 450 in total. After that, you can buy top-ups of reflections inside the app at any time, so you never run out. Your reflection balance is displayed in the app so you always know where you stand.

Is the content based on authentic Sunni scholarship?

⌄

Yes. Every piece of content in Nuria is grounded in the Holy Quran, the Hadith of the Prophet ﷺ, and the authentic tradition of Sunni Islam. Our approach draws inspiration from the classical scholars and the Sunnah, delivered with care, warmth, and respect for the faith.

Do Barakah reflections expire?

⌄

No. Barakah is a one-time in-app purchase, not a recurring subscription. Your 300 Barakah reflections — and any top-ups you purchase — are yours to use at your own pace and do not expire. Your balance is always visible inside the app.

How do I delete my account?

⌄

You can request account deletion at any time from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or by visiting our account deletion page and submitting a request. We will process your request and permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

On which devices can I use Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad) via the Apple App Store, and on Android devices via Google Play. Your account and Barakah plan work across both platforms when you sign in with the same account.
