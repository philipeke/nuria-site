About Nuria

Nuria is built with care by a small team based in Uddevalla, Sweden. We are developers and designers with a deep interest in faith and spirituality — and Nuria was founded to honour our dear Muslim friend in the studio, whose love for Islam inspired everything you see here.

Our mission is to create thoughtful, beautiful digital tools that serve the Muslim community with sincerity and respect. Every feature, every word, and every design decision is made with your spiritual journey in mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is a complete Islamic companion that brings together the full Quran with audio and 40+ translations, daily verse, duas, adhkar, Ask Nuria, reflections, Qibla, prayer times, audio, companions texts, prayer guides, prayer tracking, mosque finder, ruqyah, Halqa, Qaida, Sunnat, Live Haramain, personalization, Luxury Stone, the Islamic calendar, and progress badges in one experience.

How does Nuria work? Is it free?

⌄

Nuria is free to download and use with advertising. Every account gets the full app in 100+ languages. New users receive 15 starter reflections. Optional one-time purchases (Nuria Serene or Nuria Noor) remove ads permanently; Nuria Noor also unlocks Luxury Stone and includes 300 bonus reflections.

What changed from 4.0.0 to 4.3?

⌄

4.0.0 brought Luxury Stone (via Nuria Noor), Halqa, Qaida, Sunnat, and Live Haramain. 4.1.0 added Hiba gifts, Nuria Points, and release hardening. 4.3 makes Nuria free with ads, introduces Nuria Serene and Nuria Noor one-time purchases, Nuria Journey cloud sync, sharper Quran word-by-word audio, rewarded reflection bonuses, and polish across 100+ languages.

How do I get Luxury Stone?

⌄

Luxury Stone is included with Nuria Noor, a one-time purchase that also adds 300 bonus reflections and permanent ad-free access. Nuria Serene removes ads without Luxury Stone. Preview the palette on this website from the navbar theme selector.

What are Qaida, Sunnat, Live Haramain, and scholar path?

⌄

Nuria Qaida is a guided Quran-reading path with XP, streaks, adult and child modes. Sunnat is a searchable practice library grounded in Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim. Live Haramain opens Makkah and Madinah live streams with prayer context. Scholar path helps tune defaults for Quran, tafsir, dhikr, prayer-time settings, and guidance tone around your preferred madhhab path.

What are credits?

⌄

Reflections are your balance for personalised guidance in Nuria. Your balance is visible in the app. You can earn bonus reflections through optional rewarded ads, buy reflection top-ups, or choose Nuria Noor for 300 bonus reflections.

What are Nuria Serene and Nuria Noor?

⌄

Nuria Serene and Nuria Noor are optional one-time in-app purchases that remove banner, native, and interstitial ads permanently. Nuria Noor also unlocks the Luxury Stone theme and includes 300 bonus reflections. They do not auto-renew.

How many credits do I get?

⌄

Every new device receives 15 starter reflections once. You can add more through rewarded ads, reflection top-ups in Settings, or by choosing Nuria Noor.

Is the content based on authentic Islamic scholarship?

⌄

Yes. Every piece of content in Nuria is grounded in the Holy Quran, the Hadith of the Prophet ﷺ, and authentic Islamic teaching, delivered with care, warmth, and respect for the faith.

What happens when I run out of credits?

⌄

You can keep using Nuria's daily guidance and prayer tools as normal. Watch optional rewarded ads for bonus reflections, buy a top-up in Settings, or choose Nuria Noor for a larger reflection bundle.

How do I delete my account?

⌄

You can request account deletion from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or by visiting our account deletion page. We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

On which devices can I use Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is available on iOS via the Apple App Store and on Android via Google Play.

I found an issue or have a suggestion. How do I reach you?

Use the contact form below or email us at hello@nuria.one. We read every message and strive to respond within 1–2 business days, in shaa Allah.
