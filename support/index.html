Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is a daily Sunni Islamic guidance and reflection app. Each day you receive a handcrafted prayer, a verse from the Quran or Hadith, a role model from Islamic history, and personal spiritual reflections across 15 different categories — all rooted in authentic Sunni tradition.

Is Nuria free to use?

⌄

Yes. Nuria offers a Standard plan which is free and includes daily scripture, a daily role model, a daily personal reflection, and access to select reflection categories with a limited number of reflections each month. To unlock all 15 categories and receive more reflections, you can upgrade to Barakah.

What are reflections?

⌄

Reflections are personalised spiritual texts you request from Nuria across any of the 15 spiritual categories — such as Morning Prayer, Gratitude, Strength & Patience, and more. Each reflection is uniquely crafted to speak to your heart and situation at that moment.

What is Barakah and how do I get it?

⌄

Barakah (بركة — meaning blessing) is Nuria's premium plan. It unlocks all 15 reflection categories, gives you significantly more reflections per month, and provides early access to upcoming features. You can purchase Barakah directly inside the app on iOS or Android.

How many reflections do I get?

⌄

Standard users receive a limited number of reflections each month to explore the app. Barakah users receive a generous allowance of reflections per month — enough for daily spiritual practice. The exact number is shown inside the app at the time of purchase.

Is the content based on authentic Sunni scholarship?

⌄

Yes. Every piece of content in Nuria is grounded in the Holy Quran, the Hadith of the Prophet ﷺ, and the authentic tradition of Sunni Islam. Our approach draws inspiration from the classical scholars and the Sunnah, delivered with care, warmth, and respect for the faith.

How do I cancel Barakah?

⌄

Barakah is managed through your App Store (Apple) or Google Play account. To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings and cancel the Nuria subscription there. Your Barakah access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

⌄

You can request account deletion at any time from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account), or by visiting our account deletion page and submitting a request. We will process your request and permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

On which devices can I use Nuria?

⌄

Nuria is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad) via the Apple App Store, and on Android devices via Google Play. Your account and Barakah plan work across both platforms when you sign in with the same account.
